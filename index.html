Facts about dogs

Dogs have unique traits like a powerful sense of smell, with at least 40 times more receptors than humans, and they can even learn over 100 words and gestures. They also have a wet nose, which aids in absorbing scent chemicals, and are known to be either right or left-pawed. Additionally, dogs can see some colors and are not entirely colorblind.
